About the manufacturer

Manufacturing depth for global cooler programs.

LONFRO is supported by Guangdong Ice Energy Technology Co., Ltd., a manufacturer focused on high-performance cooling products and international production partnerships.

Production built over decades.

The company history began with plastic and hardware manufacturing in 1993, expanded into automotive component supply, and later developed specialized cooling product operations.

Today, the business combines design, engineering, tooling, molding, foaming, assembly, laboratory testing and production inspection in one manufacturing system.
